Michael Jackson

About Michael Jackson

Michael Joseph Jackson was an American singer, songwriter, dancer, and philanthropist. Dubbed the "King of Pop", he is regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century.

His parents had musical ambitions of their own. He was born in Gary, Indiana. His father was the first to notice the talent in his children. He made his recording debut at 9 years old.
